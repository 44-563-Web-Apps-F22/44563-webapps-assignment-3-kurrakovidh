My favorite Museum to visit is Navy Museum Vizag

Naval Museum offers a delightful treat for not only history buffs, but a learning experience for navy lovers as well. Located in the ENC complex of Vishakapatnam, this museum is dedicated to Indian Navy and has all the collectibles that encourage you to step back in time and pay tribute to our warriors
