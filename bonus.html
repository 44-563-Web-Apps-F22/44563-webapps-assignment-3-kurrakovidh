Kingston Kaboom

According to the Bradley University website, Kaboom! is modeled after a gargoyle that perches atop Bradley Hall on campus. And the name? Kaboom! comes from the Dave Snell, the assistant director of athletic communications and radio play-by-play announcer for the men's basketball team since 1979.
